# Quick summary of Oracc PUA blocking used as of 2025-07-31 in PCSL,
# BAU, LAK, RSP fonts
#
# *subject to change until the base Oracc fonts are all aligned to this*

F0000-F0FFF permanently assigned Oracc PUA characters

F1000-F1FFF SAC sequences; see osl/00etc/pua.tab

F2000-F27FF PC sequences; see pcsl

F2800-F2FFF PC glyph variants; see pcsls

F3000-F3888 Non-character font area for non-sign list entries, scribal doodles, etc.

F3900-FFFFD randomly used as font scratch areas
